TIC TAC TOE

Tic Tac Toe rules:

Tic Tac Toe is a really simple game. You have to figure out what your opponent plays againts you.

  • The game is played on a 3 squares by 3 squares grid.
  • One player marks X, the another one marks O. Players must put their marks into an empty squares.
  • The goal is to make 3 marks of yours in a row (horizontal, vertical or across). Who take it first, win the game!
  • When all squares is marked, and no 3 marks in a row, the game ends with tie.
